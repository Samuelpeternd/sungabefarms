About Sungabe Farms

Empowering aquaculture through innovation, mentorship, and sustainable farming.

Our Story

Sungabe Farms & Consultancy Services Nig. Ltd is a proudly Nigerian aquaculture company based in Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos. We specialize in the breeding and rearing of premium-quality catfish — from fingerlings to jumbos — and provide expert consultancy to fish farmers across the country.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize fish farming in Nigeria, Sungabe Farms is committed to delivering healthy, fast-growing catfish stock and empowering new farmers through training, mentorship, and mobile pond solutions. Our mobile tarpaulin ponds are designed for flexibility, affordability, and high yield — making aquaculture accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To raise a new generation of successful fish farmers by providing quality stock, expert guidance, and innovative farming systems that ensure profitability and sustainability.

What We Do

  • Supply of fingerlings, juveniles, and jumbo catfish
  • Construction of mobile tarpaulin fish ponds
  • Farm setup and management training
  • Consultancy for new and existing fish farmers
  • Mentorship and business development support

Meet the CEO

Sunday Gabriel is the visionary behind Sungabe Farms. With a passion for aquaculture and a heart for mentorship, he has helped dozens of aspiring farmers launch their own successful fish farming businesses. His leadership is rooted in integrity, innovation, and a deep commitment to community empowerment.
